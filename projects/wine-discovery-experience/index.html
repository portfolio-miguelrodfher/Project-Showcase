Wine Discovery Experience

Elevating exploration through a curated, user-centric wine journey

Client

International Wine Retailer

My Role

UX Lead & Product Strategist

Timeline

8 Months (2022-2023)

Focus Areas

User Experience, Personalization, Recommendation Systems

Project Overview

The Wine Discovery Experience redefines how consumers explore and select wine through a personalized, multi-channel platform that blends digital interaction with in-store experiences. By leveraging machine learning and sensory science, the platform guides users to discover wines that match their unique preferences, even when they lack the vocabulary to articulate them.

As UX Lead and Product Strategist, I orchestrated the end-to-end development of this innovative system that increased customer satisfaction by 42% and boosted average purchase value by 36%. The platform has transformed how our client engages with both novice explorers and experienced enthusiasts.

The Challenge

Our client faced significant challenges in the competitive wine retail market:

  • Overwhelming choice paralysis among customers facing extensive inventories of 2,000+ wines
  • Significant knowledge gap between expert staff and novice consumers
  • Limited ability to capture and leverage customer preference data across channels
  • Declining engagement with younger demographics intimidated by traditional wine culture

The Solution

We created an innovative multi-channel experience that makes wine discovery accessible, personalized, and engaging:

Preference Mapping Framework

Developed an intuitive system that captures taste preferences through visual and sensory cues rather than technical wine terminology. Users engage with interactive tastings that map their personal "flavor fingerprint."

AI-Powered Recommendation Engine

Built a sophisticated algorithm that correlates chemical analysis of wines with user preference data, creating increasingly accurate personalized recommendations that evolve with each interaction.

Cross-Channel Experience System

Designed seamless integration between digital platforms (mobile, web) and in-store experiences through QR-enabled shelf displays and NFC-tagged bottles that unlock personalized content.

Gamified Learning Journeys

Created modular, progressive learning experiences that build wine knowledge through exploration rather than education, including virtual tastings, regional deep dives, and food pairing challenges.

Key Insights

This project yielded valuable insights about creating transformative user experiences in complex domains:

  • Sensory translation is powerful: Converting abstract sensory experiences into visual and interactive elements dramatically reduces barriers to engagement.
  • Confidence drives exploration: When users trust a recommendation system, they become more adventurous in their choices, exploring beyond their comfort zones.
  • Physical-digital integration is essential: Seamless connection between digital discovery and physical product interaction creates the most compelling customer journeys.
  • Learning through discovery, not instruction: Users retain more knowledge when it's acquired through guided exploration rather than direct education.

Conclusion

The Wine Discovery Experience demonstrates how thoughtfully designed technology can transform traditionally intimidating experiences into accessible, engaging journeys. By focusing on personalization, visual communication, and progressive disclosure of complexity, we created a system that serves both novices and experts while driving significant business results.

This project exemplifies how sensory experiences can be effectively digitized without losing their essential character, and how the right blend of data science and experience design can create deeply human connections with products that have centuries of cultural tradition behind them.
